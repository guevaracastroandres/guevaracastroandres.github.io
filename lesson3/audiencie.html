Target Audiencie

  • Who: Our users will be people interested in outdoor activities such as sports, fishing, camping, etc. We wil also focus in people who are close to sites prone to natural disasters such as tsunami, hurricanes and high-speed winds
  • Age: 10 - 80 Years old.
  • Motivatio:Our clients will consult our website to take the necessary precautions when planning their family activities, outdoor events. They may also be informed through the comments in real time of other users about some weather conditions in specific sites.
    People who wish to receive information regarding natural disasters, will be able to see the latest reports and news of these events.
