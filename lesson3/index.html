Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information on the weather in real time tourists in colombia in real time, and share comments and useful information for travelers in the main tourist places, contributing to a better planning of our vacations.

We want our users participate in real time sharing comments about the weather in places that have a high tourist demand and at the same time sharing small recommendations such as wearing a hat, wearing a jacket, wearing boots, etc.
