Getupsun is a site focused on families and people who want to carry out outdoor activities such as sports, a barbecue or a picnic, and want to take into account the weather conditions for the preparation of your event.

On this website they can find the weather conditions of the day and other future dates. They can also check the status of weather phenomena such as hurricanes, storms and high-speed winds.

The site will also have a comments section in real time where people can share information about weather conditions that may be useful for other users.
