White Space

Apple
apple.com

There is a large amount of white space between each of the elements which facilitates the reading by the user and helps to make the design look clean and balanced.

Contrast

Apple
apple.com

In this image you can see a very colorful palette and a black typeface. These make a perfect contrast to the background, which is a gray color that has a lot of light. These contrasts make the text very easy to read and give a great appeal to the image.
